Jumping Game

    Instructions to play.

  • Click on the "Start" button to begin the game.
  • After clicking the "Start" button, the game will start, and you will see two blocks on the screen: a smaller block and a larger block.
  • Your objective is to avoid the smaller block from colliding with the larger block by jumping. To do this, click anywhere on the screen with your mouse.
  • When you click, the smaller block will perform a jump, attempting to avoid collision with the larger block.
  • Keep clicking at appropriate times to make the smaller block jump and avoid the larger block.
  • If the smaller block collides with the larger block, the game is over, and you will need to restart.
